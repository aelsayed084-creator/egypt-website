Egypt has a lot of world famous artists!

Umm Kulthum

Umm Kulthum was a celebrated 20th-century Egyptian singer and film actress, known as the "Star of the Orient" and "The Voice of Egypt". Her concerts, featuring long, emotionally charged performances, made her an iconic figure in the Arab world and a national icon in Egypt. Her legacy continues through modern technology, including the use of holograms for her performances and the creation of AI-generated voices to replicate her distinctive singing style. She was born in December 31st in Tamay AZ Zahayrah and died in February 3rd 1975 in Cairo.

Abdel Halim Hafez

Abdel Halim Hafez was a highly celebrated Egyptian singer, actor, and cultural icon known as the "Dark-Skinned Nightingale". A contemporary of Umm Kulthum and Mohammed Abdel Wahab, he was one of the "Four Greats" of Arabic music, renowned for his emotional romantic songs and patriotic anthems. Hafez's music and films continue to be popular, and his legacy is preserved through annual celebrations and the use of hologram technology for holographic performances.
